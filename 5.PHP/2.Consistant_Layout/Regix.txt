Regix For Phone NO.

"^\d{4}-?\d{7}"
         
Regix For Email

"^[A-Za-z0-9-_]+(\.[_A-Za-z0-9-]+)*@[A-Za-z0-9]+(\.[A-Za-z0-9]+)*(\.[A-Za-z]{2,4})"
